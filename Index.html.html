Clearance Instructions

  1. Login to the student portal using your student ID and password
  2. Navigate to the Clearance section and click "Apply for Clearance"
  3. Fill out the clearance form with accurate information
  4. Upload required documents and payment receipts
  5. Submit the form and wait for approval from all offices
  6. Track your clearance status in real-time
  7. Once all offices approve, download your clearance certificate
  8. For any issues, contact the clearance office during working hours

About Rockview University Clearance System

Welcome to the Rockview University Online Clearance Management System. Our platform is designed to streamline the student clearance process, making it more efficient and convenient for both students and administrative staff.

The clearance system allows students to apply for clearance online without the need to physically visit multiple offices. This digital approach saves time, reduces paperwork, and provides real-time tracking of application status.

Our Mission

To provide a seamless, transparent, and efficient clearance process that enhances the student experience while maintaining the integrity and standards of Rockview University.

How It Works

Students can log in using their university credentials, submit clearance applications, and track the approval process through four key offices: Reception, Admission, Systems, and Accounts. Once all offices have approved the application, students can download their clearance certificate directly from the system.

Benefits

  • 24/7 access to the clearance system
  • Real-time status updates
  • Reduced waiting time and paperwork
  • Environmentally friendly digital process
  • Secure and confidential handling of student information
